Pizza

Description

This classic homemade pizza features a crisp, chewy crust topped with rich tomato sauce, melted cheese, and your favorite toppings. It’s easy to prepare and perfect for family dinners or weekend treats. The dough rises beautifully, giving you that authentic pizzeria-style texture right from your own oven.

Ingredients

  • 2 ½ cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 packet (7g) active dry yeast
  • ¾ cup warm water
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • ½ cup pizza sauce
  • 1 ½ cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • Toppings of your choice (pepperoni, vegetables, etc.)

Steps

  1. In a small bowl, mix warm water, sugar, and yeast. Let it sit until foamy (about 5 minutes).
  2. In a large bowl, combine flour and salt. Add the yeast mixture and olive oil.
  3. Knead the dough for 8–10 minutes until smooth and elastic.
  4. Cover and let the dough rise for 1 hour, or until doubled in size.
  5. Preheat the oven to 220°C (425°F).
  6. Roll out the dough into a circle and spread pizza sauce evenly over it.
  7. Add cheese and toppings.
  8. Bake for 12–15 minutes, or until the crust is golden and the cheese is bubbly.
